What to Expect

For individuals interested in treatment, the following is what you can expect:

Phone Introduction

As a potential patient, you will call Dr. Mohandessi and arrange for a phone introduction. During a brief phone introduction, Dr. Mohandessi will inquire about your symptoms and reason for seeking treatment. He will orient you to his practice and schedule. Based on Dr. Mohandessi's availability, you will be able to schedule an Initial Evaluation appointment. After arranging your appointment, please visit the Forms section and review the New Patient Forms prior to your appointment.

Initial Evaluation

Dr. Mohandessi's Initial Evaluation may last between 60 and 90 minutes. Please arrive at least 15 minutes prior to your appointment to complete the necessary Forms. During the Initial Evaluation, Dr. Mohandessi will ask about your personal, medical, psychiatric, and family histories while listening to your concerns. Your openness with Dr. Mohandessi will allow him to understand your difficulties, come to a diagnosis and form an appropriate treatment plan.

Diagnostic Phase

Depending on the case, Dr. Mohandessi may require one to two additional 50 minutes sessions to complete a thorough evaluation of your case. During the Diagnostic Phase, Dr. Mohandessi will gather more information about your symptoms and difficulties to make an appropriate diagnosis. He may also recommend obtaining prior medical records or refer you for a medical consultation and work-up. This Phase is also an opportunity for you to get to know Dr. Mohandessi's practice and decide if it is a good fit for you. By the end of the Diagnostic Phase, Dr. Mohandessi will share with you his findings and treatment recommendations. If you both agree, Dr. Mohandessi will become your psychiatrist and you will enter treatment with him.

Short-term Treatment Phase

After entering treatment with Dr. Mohandessi, The Short-term Treatment Phase will focus on amelioration and management of the symptoms that led you to treatment. This Phase of treatment may last several weeks to months and involves regular appointments. The Short-term Treatment Phase may vary from patient-to-patient and include medication management and/or psychotherapy. During this phase, you and Dr. Mohandessi will be able to discuss your goals and expectations for treatment. Overall, Dr. Mohandessi will work with you towards bringing your symptoms under control.

Long-term Treatment Phase

Once your symptoms are brought under control and the short-term goals of your treatment have been met, you may enter the Long-term Treatment Phase. This Phase of treatment may last between several months to years and also consists of regular appointments. The focus of treatment will transition to life issues such as your relationships, self-awareness, self-esteem, productivity, individual efficacy and life satisfaction. Dr. Mohandessi strives to guide his patients through stages of greater understanding and insight toward lasting change and enhanced mental well-being.

If you are interested in treatment with Dr. Mohandessi, please visit the Forms section and call the office to make an appointment.
